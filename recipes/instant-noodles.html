Instant noodles

Description

For those lazy days where you don't have the energy to cook a full meal, this dish is the perfect balance between taste and effort.

Cooking this dish is simple, just follow these steps:

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles
  • Eggs
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Chop the scallions into thin slices
  2. Boil the water and cook the noodles for 2 minutes
  3. Add the eggs into the noodles and stir for 30 seconds
  4. Serve and enjoy immediately
