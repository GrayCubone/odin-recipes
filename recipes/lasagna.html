Lasagna

Description

This dish is a vegetarian lasagna that will warm your heart and stomach on a cold winter day.

Cooking this dish is simple, just follow these steps:

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Aubergine

Steps

  1. Fry the aubergine in a pan
  2. Cook the bechamel sauce
  3. Combine the ingredients in a pan, layering the sauce, past and aubergine
  4. Cook in oven at 400 degrees for 45 minutes
