Carbonara

Description

This dish is a delicious pasta dish that you can cook with just a few ingredients and very quickly!

Cooking this dish is simple, just follow these steps:

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water
  2. Cook the pancetta in a pan
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add the cheese
  4. Combine ingredients in pan and add salt to taste
